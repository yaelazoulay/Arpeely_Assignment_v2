Optimization strategy

This flow is redesigned from the assignment funnel: strong starts, a sharp Q1→Q2 drop from configuration fatigue, and a signup wall that must feel fair. Below is the concise plan embodied in this page.

Diagnosis
  • Intent gap: “Free AI humanizer” searchers expect paste → fast signal; eight micro-settings before any output feels like product onboarding.
  • Where people leave: The steepest cliff is after the first question when granular English / tone / vocabulary screens stack up.
  • Trust at the wall: A blurred preview after a long quiz can read as bait-and-switch unless copy and timing align with the promise.
What we changed
Compressed quizFour steps: outcome, combined tone, English variant, intensity slider. Same quiz format, fewer exit points.
Honest heroSignup unlocks the full rewrite; preview is blurred by design. No “unlimited free” over-promise.
Progress & hintsBar + live hints so each tap feels like movement toward a result, not endless config.
Credible comparisonIllustrative before/after labels—not precise fake detector percentages that invite skepticism.
Modal as payoffCTA restates what they get now; one line on why signup exists (quality, sync).
